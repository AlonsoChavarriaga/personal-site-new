Pokemon QR Codes

Vue.js - Webpack - AWS - Responsive - SASS

SUMMARY

I created this site to help myself and others keep track of which QR codes they've scanned. There are 284 different QR codes right now, with more on the way.


The site needed to be small and load quickly, so I used Vue to render the page, and implemented lazy loading on the images. To improve adoption rates and decrease friction, I opted out of adding any sort of authentication; instead, the site works entirely on local storage.

FEATURES

  • All 284 Pokemon
  • Uses local storage
  • Lazy loading images
  • Fully responsive
